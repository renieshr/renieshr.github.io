About Me

Hello! I'm rajesh, a passionate photographer based in bengaluru. My journey into photography began from childwood when I discovered the magic of freezing moments in album captured by my father. I specialize in landscape, portrait, baby shoot and wedding photography, always striving to tell a unique story with every click of the shutter.

My approach is natural, candid and artistic. I believe that the best photographs are those that evoke emotion and connect with the viewer on a deeper level. When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring new places, hiking, rappelling.

Feel free to browse my portfolio and don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or would like to discuss a project.
